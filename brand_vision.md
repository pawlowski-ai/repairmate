## 3) Tone of Voice (with sliders)

- **Direct ■■■■■ Soft** (plain, but friendly)
- **Supportive ■■■■■ Tough-love** (motivating, not macho)
- **Everyday ■■■■■ Technical** (avoid jargon)
- **Confident ■■■■□ Humble** (no hype, just competence)

**Do:** short sentences, everyday verbs, second person (“you”).

**Don’t:** lecture, shame, techno-babble, passive voice.

**Brand lexicon:** fix, mend, check, diagnose, guide, step, try, tighten, replace, done.

**Words to avoid:** guru, hacky, complicated acronyms, “error code 0x…”.

## 8) Microcopy Patterns & Safety

- **Time estimates:** *Estimated time: 5–10 min*
- **Tools:** *You’ll need: screwdriver (Phillips), towel*
- **Warnings:** *Turn off power at the breaker before you start.*
- **Accessibility:** write at ~6–8th grade reading level; avoid tiny type; sentence case.

## 12) Guardrails (consistency)

- Keep messages **short, active, specific**.
- Use **one CTA per screen**.
- **Lime = action** only (primary CTAs/success).
- Never imply guaranteed safety or professional certification; use *nudges*, not absolutes.
